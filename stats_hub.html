What to notice

The mean is pulled toward the tail in skewed data. The median resists outliers. When mean > median → right-skewed. When mean < median → left-skewed.

Mean = Σxᵢ / n    Median = middle value when sorted

How to read a boxplot

The box spans Q1 to Q3 (middle 50%). Whiskers extend to the furthest non-outlier point. An outlier is anything beyond 1.5×IQR from the box edges.

IQR = Q3 − Q1    Outlier if x < Q1 − 1.5·IQR or x > Q3 + 1.5·IQR

The 68-95-99.7 Rule

In a normal distribution: 68% within 1σ, 95% within 2σ, 99.7% within 3σ. A z-score tells you how many standard deviations from the mean.

z = (x − μ) / σ

Center vs. Spread

Set both σ equal and slide the means apart — that's pure location shift. Then set means equal and change σ — that's pure scale change. These are the two fundamental properties of any distribution.
